The Game
What Is
Brawl Stars?

Brawl Stars is a fast-paced multiplayer battle game developed by Supercell — the studio behind Clash of Clans and Clash Royale. Launched in 2017, it has grown into one of the most popular mobile games worldwide, with tens of millions of active players across all platforms.

Players choose from a roster of unique characters called Brawlers, each with their own attacks, special abilities, and playstyles. Matches are designed to be quick — most last only a few minutes — making it ideal for mobile gaming sessions on the go.

Whether you enjoy casual play or competitive ranked battles, Brawl Stars delivers an experience that stays fresh thanks to regular updates, new brawlers, seasonal events, and a thriving esports scene.

Players also explore private server experiences like Nulls Brawl Mod to test unlimited resources, unlocked brawlers, and experimental features not available in the official version.
